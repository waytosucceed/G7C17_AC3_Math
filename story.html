In the bustling city of Routeville, traffic congestion was becoming a major issue. The mayor decided it was time for a redesign of the city's busiest intersection. Ava, a highly skilled traffic engineer, was given the responsibility of this crucial project. Ava knew that precision and careful planning were key to success.

Ava’s successful redesign of Routeville’s intersection demonstrated her expertise in geometric principles. Her precise application of corresponding and vertical angles, as well as complementary and supplementary angles, led to a smoother traffic flow and improved city navigation.
